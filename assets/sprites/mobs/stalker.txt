# The only thing that kills you unseen, so the frame you DO see it has to land
# in one glance. Claws up (`^`), legs trailing (`~`): a long reaching thing that
# is never mistaken for the upright Warden. It gathers itself on the second frame
# -- limbs pulled in, about to spring.
#
# Bright magenta head is deliberate and is the one warm cell on it. Magenta
# appears nowhere else in the bestiary; when it enters your lantern you should
# feel the colour before you read the shape.
#
# The head used to be `(0)` and both characters were borrowed:
#   `0` -- the digits 0-9 are the DAMAGE NUMBERS' alphabet (design.md §14) and
#          may not be shared with anything that stands on the field.
#   `( )` -- rotting flesh speaks in parentheses (the Ghoul). A stalker is not
#          rotting; it is patient.
# `<¤>` is unclaimed, and it is a better drawing: a lamp of an eye held in
# mandibles, rather than a hole.
