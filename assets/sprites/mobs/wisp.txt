# THIS SPRITE WAS DRAWN OUT OF THE PLAYER'S OWN BOLT. It was `(*)` over `'.'`:
# a bright-red `*` with a `.` under it. The Warden's starting weapon fires a `*`
# that fades to a `.` (render.ts). So from 12:00 -- the exact minute the field is
# fullest -- the one enemy that IGNORES ENEMY COLLISION, and therefore is the one
# thing that reaches you through the pile, was drawn with the same two characters
# as the projectile you fire at it. Mistaking your own bolt for an incoming enemy
# is a failure of design.md §0 item 4, "you can see what is about to touch you."
#
# It also wore the Ghoul's parentheses. Rotting flesh speaks in `( )`; a wisp is
# not flesh.
#
# Blood spirits speak in BRACES: curling, weightless, unclaimed by anything else.
# The shell flickers out on the second frame and the bead of blood hangs there
# alone -- a will-o'-wisp guttering. The flicker IS the animation; it needs no
# extra glyph to sell it.
