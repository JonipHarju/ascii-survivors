#
# The Warden is a hooded, cloaked figure now with a lantern that swings as he
# walks. The head is a `@` set in a cowl -- a dark `▄▄▄` crown over a face framed
# by `▐ ▌` -- and the body is a solid `█` core. The VOLUME is glyph density, not
# colour: the luminance ladder (§9) still holds, the whole figure is white so the
# eye lands on the player first, and the `@` at his face stays the one bright-white
# `W` in the game, popping against the darker cowl that frames it.
#
# Glyphs are DELIBERATELY only the ones John has already confirmed render on his
# canvas single-width (block elements -- his XP bar draws `▁..█` -- plus `◆` and
# the player's own `@ / \`). No rounded corners, no box-diagonals: this is the most
# important sprite in the game and it may not depend on a glyph I haven't seen draw.
